# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Two distinct user groups:

1. **Citizens / victims** — submit distress calls during emergencies. They are stressed, in a hurry, and need an interface that gets out of their way. Primary actions: locate on map, describe the situation, submit.
2. **Admins / operators** — manage incoming calls, assign rescue centers, track response status. They need efficiency, data density, and clear status overviews. Primary actions: triage calls, dispatch resources, monitor resolution.

## Product Purpose

An emergency response platform connecting disaster victims with rescue and supply centers across Vietnam. Citizens report emergencies (flood, fire, landslide, storm) by dropping a pin on a map and describing their situation. AI suggests relevant supplies. Admins triage calls, dispatch rescue resources, and track resolution. Success is measured by response time and lives saved.

## Brand Personality

**Urgent, Trustworthy, Human.** The interface conveys urgency without panic, authority without coldness. Every visual decision prioritizes clarity under stress.

## Anti-references

No specific anti-references. Avoid generic admin-panel aesthetics — this is not a Bootstrap dashboard. The urgency of the domain should be felt through the interface, not pasted on with decorative elements.

## Design Principles

- **Clarity under pressure.** Every element earns its place. No decoration that doesn't serve the task. When someone's home is flooding, the interface doesn't get in the way.
- **Speed by default.** The primary path — submit a call, triage calls — is one click or less from anywhere. No wasted motion.
- **Trust through precision.** Status badges, urgency scores, timestamps, and location data are always visible and always accurate. Uncertainty is communicated honestly.
- **Voice is Vietnamese.** The language is Vietnamese, the data is Vietnamese geography, and the mental model matches how Vietnamese emergency services actually work.
- **Works in any light.** Dark mode for control rooms and night ops; light mode for field use in daylight.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. This is an emergency platform; information must be legible under any condition, including screen readers, low vision, and stressful environments. Color is never the sole conveyor of meaning (urgency, status).
